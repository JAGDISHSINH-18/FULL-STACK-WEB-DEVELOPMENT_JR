Royal Challengers Bangalore (often abbreviated as RCB) are a professional franchise cricket team based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, 
that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). It was founded in 2008 by United Spirits and named after the company's liquor brand
Royal Challenge.
The Royal Challengers have not won the IPL but finished runner-up on three occasions between 2009 and 2016.
